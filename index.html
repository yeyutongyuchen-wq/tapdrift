Simple to learn. Impossible to master.

Tap Drift brings the thrill of arcade racing back to its purest form. Stripping away complex controls and gear shifts, the game focuses on a single, crucial mechanic: timing.

The car accelerates automatically. Your job is to guide it through treacherous, randomly generated tracks. It's a test of reflexes and rhythm designed for quick sessions and endless replayability.

Pro Tips for High Scores

  • 1. Don't over-steer: Short, controlled taps are often better than long holds. The car turns sharper than you think.
  • 2. Anticipate the curve: Look ahead of the car, not at the car. Start your drift slightly before the corner begins.
  • 3. Find your rhythm: The generated tracks often follow a pattern. Once you find the rhythm of the zig-zags, you'll flow through them.
